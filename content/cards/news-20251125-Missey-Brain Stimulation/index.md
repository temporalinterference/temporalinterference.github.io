---
title: New Publication "Non-Invasive TI Stimulation of the Hippocampus Suppresses Epileptic Biomarkers in Patients with Epilepsy"
---
November 25, 2025

In a recent paper published in Brain Stimulation, the use of non-invasive temporal interference (TI) stimulation for epilepsy therapy was investigated across carrier frequencies ranging from 1 to 9 kHz.
